SECTION 4 – COVERAGES
**4.1 Bodily Injury & Property Damage Liability (Coverage A)**
We will pay damages for bodily injury or property damage for which an insured person becomes legally responsible because of an accident arising out of the ownership, maintenance, or use of a covered auto. We will settle or defend, as we consider appropriate, any claim or suit asking for these damages. Our duty to settle or defend ends when our limit of liability has been exhausted by payment of judgments or settlements.

– **Supplementary Payments**—In addition to our limit of liability, we will pay on behalf of an insured person:
• Up to \$250 for the cost of bail bonds;
• Premiums on appeal bonds;
• Interest accruing after a judgment is entered;
• Loss of earnings up to \$200 per day when attending trials at our request.

**4.2 Collision (Coverage B)**
We will pay for loss to your covered auto caused by collision with another object or overturn. We will pay up to the actual cash value of the vehicle less any applicable deductible.

**4.3 Comprehensive (Coverage C)**
We will pay for loss to your covered auto not caused by collision, including but not limited to fire, theft, vandalism, hail, flood, and contact with an animal.

**4.4 Uninsured / Underinsured Motorist (Coverage D)**
We will pay compensatory damages which an insured person is legally entitled to recover from the owner or operator of an uninsured motor vehicle because of bodily injury sustained by an insured person.

**4.5 Medical Payments (Coverage E)**
We will pay reasonable expenses incurred for necessary medical and funeral services to or for an insured person who sustains bodily injury caused by accident.

**4.6 Personal Injury Protection (PIP) (Coverage F)**
Where required or elected, we will provide no‑fault benefits for medical expenses, loss of income, and essential services as required by applicable state law.

**4.7 Rental Reimbursement (Coverage G)**
We will reimburse up to \$35 per day, to a maximum of \$1,050 per loss, for rental charges incurred when your covered auto is withdrawn from use for more than 24 hours because of a covered loss.

**4.8 Roadside Assistance (Coverage H)**
We will pay reasonable expenses for towing, battery boost, flat‑tire service, fuel delivery, and lock‑out assistance for your covered auto, up to the benefit limits shown in Section 8.

SECTION 5 – EXCLUSIONS
This Policy does **not** apply to any of the following, regardless of contributing causes:

1. **Intentional Acts** – Bodily injury or property damage intentionally caused by an insured person.
2. **Racing or Speed Contests** – Loss arising out of participation in racing, speed, demolition, or stunting contests.
3. **Wear & Tear** – Gradual deterioration, mechanical failure, rust, or corrosion.
4. **Commercial Use** – While the covered auto is carrying persons or property for a fee (including rideshare or delivery) unless you have purchased a rideshare endorsement.
5. **War & Nuclear Hazard** – Loss caused by war, rebellion, or nuclear reaction.
6. **Personal Property** – Loss to tapes, records, data discs, or personal items carried in or on the vehicle.
7. **Named Driver Exclusion** – Any person specifically excluded by endorsement.
8. **Unauthorized Use** – Operation of the vehicle by any person without a valid driver’s license or outside the scope of your permission.

SECTION 6 – CONDITIONS
**6.1 Policy Changes** – This policy contains all agreements between you and us. Its terms may not be altered except by written endorsement issued by us.

**6.2 Premium Payment** – If your premium payment is not honored, coverage may be cancelled back to the due date subject to state law.

**6.3 Your Duties After Accident or Loss** – In the event of an accident, you must give us prompt notice and provide all cooperation reasonably required, including:
a. Securing names, addresses, and phone numbers of involved parties and witnesses;
b. Notifying police when a hit‑and‑run driver is involved;
c. Protecting the vehicle from further damage;
d. Submitting to statements under oath if requested.

**6.4 Fraud or Misrepresentation** – This policy is void if you or an insured person has intentionally concealed or misrepresented any material fact or circumstance.

**6.5 Cancellation & Non‑Renewal** – You may cancel this policy at any time by providing advance notice. We may cancel only under conditions permitted by state law, such as non‑payment of premium or driver‑license suspension.

**6.6 Subrogation** – If we make payment under this Policy, we shall be subrogated to all rights of recovery of an insured person against another party.

**6.7 Appraisal & Arbitration** – If we and you do not agree on the amount of loss, either may demand an appraisal. Unresolved liability disputes under Uninsured Motorist coverage may be submitted to binding arbitration.
